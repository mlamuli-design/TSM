About TSM

TSM Granite was founded in 2006 with limited resources, driven by challenges that inspired innovation in granite processing and installation. Our motto, "Every Project A Masterpiece," became a culture of excellence.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of granite products and services globally.

Our Mision

Delivering quality granite solutions with excellence, innovation, and reliability.

Over the years, we have grown steadily, forming strong partnerships with South Africa’s largest stone suppliers, helping us evolve from a small company to an industry leader. In 2014, we expanded to Botswana, ensuring consistent efficiency and excellence in every branch.

TSM-Granite Products

The company’s main line of operation is the fabrication of stone products for use in households, including the following:

Kitchen & Bathroom Worktops

We create custom granite worktops for kitchens and bathrooms, providing both elegance and functionality that elevate any space.

Bathtub & Fireplace Surrounds

Our granite surrounds add sophistication and durability to your bathtubs and fireplaces, offering high-quality finishes for a lasting impression.

Steamer Room & Kitchen Wall Cladding

Granite wall cladding for kitchens and steamer rooms, enhancing both style and functionality while providing a durable finish.

Staircases & Window Sills

Our granite staircases and window sills bring elegance and durability to your home, with a focus on quality craftsmanship.

Coffee Tables & Dinner Tables

Wide range of granite tables, from coffee tables to dining tables, each crafted to complement your home’s aesthetic.

Shower Walls

We fabricate stunning and durable granite shower walls, ensuring a stylish and easy-to-maintain bathroom experience.
